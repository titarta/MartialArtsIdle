# Reincarnation

## Overview

Reincarnation is the **prestige mechanic** — the player resets the world in exchange for permanent bonuses that carry forward into future lives.

---

## How It Works

1. Player can **voluntarily die** (suicide) at any time
2. **Knowledge is retained** — the player remembers everything from past lives
3. The **world resets** — including a new seed that governs drop pools
4. Certain things **persist** across reincarnations (see below)

---

## World Seed

- The world has a **seed** that limits which drops can appear
- Reincarnating generates a new seed → access to different drops/items
- Encourages multiple runs to discover all content

---

## What Persists

| Persistent | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Knowledge** | Player remembers laws, recipes, world layout (meta-knowledge) |
| **Element Affinity** (mental) | Passive bonus toward element |
| **Element Affinity** (physical) | Physical resonance bonus |
| **Talent** | Cultivation speed / power multiplier |
| **Soul-bound Artefacts** | Artefacts bound to soul survive reset (with lower bound limit) |
| **Starting cultivation boost** | Unlocked at higher realms — begin future runs ahead |

---

## Affinities & Interactions

- Affinities from different reincarnations can **relate / combine**
- Combining complementary affinities may unlock new effects (TBD)

---

## Artefact Soul Binding

- Specific artefacts can be **"glued" to the soul**
- They persist through reincarnation
- **Lower bound applies** — only artefacts above a minimum quality/realm can be soul-bound
